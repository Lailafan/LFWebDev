Fluffy Banana Pancakes

Start your day right with these golden, fluffy banana pancakes - a perfect balance of sweetness and warmth!

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter

Instructions

  1. Mash the banana in a bowl.
  2. Add egg, milk, vanilla, and melted butter. Mix well.
  3. In another bowl, combine flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  4. Mix dry and wet ingredients until combined.
  5. Heat a pan and cook pancakes until golden brown.
  6. Serve with your favorite toppings.
